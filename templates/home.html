House Pricing Data Overview

This dataset contains detailed information about houses, including their physical features and sale details. It is used to train a machine learning model that predicts the sale price of a house.

Key Columns in the Dataset:

  • Flat Area (in Sqft): The total built-up area of the house.
  • No of Bedrooms: Total number of bedrooms in the house.
  • No of Bathrooms: Total number of bathrooms.
  • No of Floors: Number of floors or stories in the house.
  • Age of House (in Years): How old the house is.
  • Sale Price: The actual selling price of the house (target variable).
  • Other columns: Additional information such as location, condition, renovation, and lot area.

The goal of this data is to predict the sale price of a house using these key features.
